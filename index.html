FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a solar system cost? +
The cost depends on your energy needs and system size. A typical 3-bedroom home system starts from ₦2.5M. We provide free quotes after an energy audit.
How long does installation take? +
Residential installations typically take 2–3 days. Commercial projects vary from 1–4 weeks depending on scale and complexity.
Do you offer warranties and maintenance? +
Yes. Panels come with 25-year performance warranty, batteries with 10-year warranty, and inverters with 5-year warranty. We also offer comprehensive maintenance plans.
Can I add batteries later? +
Absolutely. We design systems to be scalable so you can start with solar-only and add battery storage later as your budget allows.
Do you handle permits and approvals? +
Yes, we manage all necessary permits and comply with NERC regulations. We handle the paperwork so you don't have to.
